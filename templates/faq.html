Frequently Asked Questions

General

What is the date and time of the prom?

Wednesday, June 24, 2026 from 5:30 pm - 10:30 pm

Where will the prom be held?

Ajax Convention Centre

What is the theme of the prom?

Twilight Garden

Is there a specific dress code?

Semi-Casual

Tickets

How much do tickets cost?

$105 + School Cash Online Fee

Where and when can we purchase tickets?

Tickets will be made available on School Cash online as of March 16, 2026

Can tickets be bought at the door, or is pre-purchase required?

Tickets must be pre-purchased

What is the last date to purchase tickets?

May 29, 2026

Guests

Can I bring a guest who doesn’t attend this school?

Yes. Your guest must be a current Ontario High School Student.

Is there an age limit for guests?

Yes. 18 years.

Do guests need to be pre-registered or approved?

Yes. Forms can be found within this website, or in the Main Office. Please return it by May 22, and wait for approval.

Can I attend without a date?

Yes! Of Course!

Are tickets transferable if someone can’t attend?

No. Tickets are not transferrable.

Logistics

Can we leave the event and come back later?

No. There is no re-entry once you have left the Grad Social Area.

Are bags or purses allowed, and will there be security checks?

Yes. All bags and coats will be checked on entry.

Food & Drinks

Will food and drinks be provided? If so, what type?

Yes. There will be a buffet dinner and dessert, tea, coffee, non-alcoholic mocktails, candy, and other treats.

Are there options for students with dietary restrictions?

Yes. Please fill out the Food Allergy/ Dietary Restriction Google Form to provide details there.

Is there a seating plan?

Yes. You will select your seats once tickets have been purchased.

Preparation

What happens if I lose my ticket?

You do not need your physical ticket to enter Grad Social. Please ensure any guests bring ID with them.
